Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 31, 2024

CraftMyHaven.in takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information when you visit our website.

Our website is not for children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.
This policy may change, and we will notify you by posting the updated version on our website. Please review it periodically to stay informed.
